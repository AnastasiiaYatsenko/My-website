About me

An accountant, who has an anormous desire to be a front-end developer

In such a difficult time for me, SheCodes provided free classes for Ukrainian women. That's how I ended up in programming, which dragged me into its nets. Now I learn something new every day. At the moment, I am a beginner front-end developer, but I am looking for new opportunities for learning and cooperation.
